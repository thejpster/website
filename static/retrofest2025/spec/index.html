SPEC Benchmarks

Here's a table of all the machines, and their performance in the SPEC 95 benchmarks.

SPECint 95 measures integer performance and SPECfp 95 measures floating-point performance - the numbers are the performance multiple compared to some reference machine. I'm also giving the "SPEC score per GHz", showing how much work a given processor can do per clock cycle. This allows us to compare processor architectures, even if the processors have different clock speeds.

I was unable to find results for the two Arm machines, but I suspect they would be very bad.

Processor Year SPECint 95 SPECint 95 per GHz SPECfp 95 SPECfp 95 per GHz
HP PA-8500 @ 400 MHz 1998 31.8 79.5 52.4 131
Intel Pentium II @ 450 MHz 1998 17.6 39.1 13.1 29.1
UltraSPARC-II @ 450 MHz 1997 19.7 43.7 27.9 62.0
Alpha 21164A @ 500 MHz 1996 15.0 30.0 20.4 40.8

Intel Pentium PRO @ 150 MHz 1996 6.08 40.5 5.42 36.1
