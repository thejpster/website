UNIX Exhibitors at RetroFest 2025

Here are the exhibitors taking part in our little UNIX corner of RetroFest 2025.

Jonathan 'theJPster' Pallant

Jonathan (or JP to his friends) is an Embedded Systems developer and Rust evangelist by day, but has recently fallen into the world of 1990's UNIX hardware and software.

You can e-mail JP on "retrofest AT thejpster.org.uk". Or you can find him on Bluesky (@thejpster.org.uk) or on Mastodon (thejpster@hachyderm.io). He also runs https://thejpster.org.uk, which posts blogs about trains and Rust and sometimes his adventures with UNIX workstations.

JP brought the Silicon Graphics POWER Indigo 2, Sun Ultra 80, HP Visualize B132L+ and HP Visualize C3000. He also owns an Acorn RiscPC 700, Commodore 128D, Acorn BBC Master, Commodore Amiga 500, two Atari 520STs and a lot of consoles.

Peter 'El Flib/Flibble/Marutan' Howkins

Peter is a Software Engineer by trade. As an enjoyer of all things 'Acorn Computers' and 'Old UNIX' it seemed natural to bring along a machine where those two overlap. He is a co-developer of the RPCEmu emulator, a free recreation of the Acorn Risc PC. He is also one of the maintainers of the open-source release of the Common Desktop Environment (CDE).

Peter brought the Acorn R260, Acorn Risc PC and RPCEmu machines, though not all of them are running UNIX! His collection includes 20 or so Acorn machines and a dozen or more other items.
